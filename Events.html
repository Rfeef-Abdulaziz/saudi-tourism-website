Cultural Events

Riyadh

Riyadh Season

Riyadh Season is one of the largest entertainment festivals in Saudi Arabia, featuring concerts, international performances, and family-friendly events throughout the city.

Diriyah

Diriyah Festival

The Diriyah Festival is a celebration of the rich cultural heritage of Saudi Arabia, featuring music, art, traditional food, and historical events at the UNESCO World Heritage site of Diriyah.

Al-Ula

Winter at Tantora is a spectacular winter festival held in Al-Ula, featuring classical music, art exhibitions, and a blend of culture and history against the stunning desert backdrop.

Formula E in Jeddah is part of the electric car racing championship, held in the vibrant city of Jeddah. The event features high-speed races on the Red Sea coastline, attracting motorsport fans from around the world.

Tourism Events

Red Sea Coast

Red Sea Project

The Red Sea Project is a transformative tourism initiative aimed at creating a world-class, eco-friendly resort destination along Saudi Arabia’s western coast. It will feature luxury resorts, pristine islands, and vibrant marine ecosystems.

Learn More

Jazan

Jazan Season

Jazan Season showcases the region's natural beauty and rich culture, with events like artistic performances, sports activities, and the famous Hareed Fish Festival. Visitors can explore stunning attractions such as Farasan Islands and Wadi Lajab.
